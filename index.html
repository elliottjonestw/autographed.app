Views

Grid and Table, your way

Browse your collection as a photo-forward card grid or a data-dense table. Switch between them instantly. Customize how many columns appear, and choose which fields show on each card.

  • Grid view: photo cards with signer, cert, value, and ROI at a glance
  • Table view: sortable rows with all fields, clickable signer and title links
  • 3–8 columns per row, saved between visits
  • Custom order: drag items into any sequence you like

Authentication

All cert types supported

Log which company authenticated each piece. Cert numbers become clickable links that open the authentication company's official verification page and copy the number to your clipboard in one tap.

Search & Filter

Find anything, instantly

Live search across signers, characters, films, tags, and notes. Layer in multi-select filters for cert type, item type, condition, signing event, location, and more, all active simultaneously.

  • Live search: results update on every keystroke
  • Multi-select filters: mix and match tags, cert type, condition, acquisition method, and more
  • Sort by date, name, value, paid, ROI, or your own custom order

Sharing & Export

Share/export everything

Generate a shareable link that lets anyone browse your collection read-only in their own browser, no app install needed. Export to JSON for backup or CSV for spreadsheets. Print a beautifully laid-out A4 catalog.

  • Share link: one click creates a public read-only URL for your collection
  • JSON export / import: full backup including photos, restore on any device
  • CSV export: open in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets
  • Print layout: A4 card grid with stats header, ready to print
  • Merge import: combine collections without overwriting existing data

Item Detail

Every detail, on display

Tap any card to open a full detail view. Multiple photos with a thumbnail strip to flip through. Auto-generated descriptions ready to paste into a listing. ROI tracking that updates live as values change.

  • Multiple photos per item: tap to browse, pinch/scroll to zoom
  • Auto-description: generates a ready-to-paste listing description from your fields
  • ROI tracking: see your return on every piece, colored green or red
  • Clickable tags: tap a tag to filter the gallery instantly
  • Presentation mode: full-screen slideshow for displaying on a TV

Everything else

Packed with features

Live Currency Conversion

Record values in your local currency, display in any of 152 national currencies. Live exchange rates fetched automatically and cached for 1 hour.

Multi-Language Support

Full UI translations included. The app auto-detects your browser language on first visit, with no setup required.

Dark & Light Mode

Toggle between a rich dark theme and a clean light theme. Your preference is saved between visits.

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive layout. Two-column grid on phones, full filter and search support, touch-friendly throughout.

Tags & Metadata

Add freeform tags to any item. Log signing event, signing date, acquisition method, condition, and physical storage location.

Flexible Photo Formats

Nine aspect ratios covering 8×10, 5×7, 4×6, index cards, trading cards, and square formats. Crop, contain, or fill with a blurred background.

Privacy Mode

Blur all monetary values with one toggle, perfect for displaying your collection without revealing what you paid.

20+ Item Types

Photos, posters, jerseys, bats, helmets, records, books, comics, trading cards, and more, across sports, film & TV, music, and books.

Wikipedia & IMDb Links

Signer names and titles automatically link to Wikipedia or IMDb, your choice. One click from any card to the right page.
