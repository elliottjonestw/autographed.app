Your data will be public

Generating a share link uploads your entire collection — item details, photos, paid amounts, estimated values, and certificate numbers — to dpaste.com, a public paste service. Anyone who obtains the link can view all of this information.

If your gallery contains financial details or personal information you wouldn't want others to see, do not proceed. Instead, consider using Export Collection to save a .json file and share it privately.

Share links expire after 365 days.
