Grid & Table Views

Browse as a photo-forward card grid or a data-dense table. Choose 3–8 columns, toggle which fields show on each card, and arrange items in any custom order.

Authentication Certificates

Log the cert company for each piece. Cert numbers become clickable links to the authenticator's official verification page and copy to your clipboard in one tap.

Live Search & Filters

Search across signers, characters, films, tags, and notes on every keystroke. Layer multi-select filters for cert type, condition, item type, signing event, location, and more.

Value & ROI Tracking

Log what you paid and your estimated market value for every item. See return on investment colored green or red. Link any item to eBay sold listings to check real market prices.

Rich Item Detail

Every item gets a full detail view with multiple photos, zoom, auto-generated listing descriptions, and clickable tags. Plus a full-screen slideshow for displaying on a TV.

Share & Export

Generate a read-only share link, export to JSON or CSV, and print a formatted A4 catalog. Merge imports let you combine collections without overwriting existing items.

20+ Item Types

Photos, posters, jerseys, bats, helmets, records, books, comics, trading cards, and more, across sports, film & TV, music, and books.

Tags & Metadata

Add freeform tags to any item. Log signing event, signing date, acquisition method, condition, and physical storage location.

Live Currency Conversion

Record values in your local currency, display in any of 152 national currencies. Live exchange rates fetched automatically and cached for 1 hour.

Privacy Mode

Blur all monetary values with one toggle, perfect for displaying your collection without revealing what you paid.

Cloud Sync

Create a free account to back up your collection and restore it on any device. Your data lives locally by default — no account required to get started.

Multi-Language Support

Full UI translations in English, Spanish, French, German, and Traditional Chinese. The app auto-detects your browser language on first visit.
